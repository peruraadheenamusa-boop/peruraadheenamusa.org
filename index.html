Welcome

Perur Aadheenam USA is the American chapter of the historic 500-year-old Perur Aadheenam in Coimbatore, India. Our mission is to preserve Tamil Saivite culture, support community education, and connect the Tamil diaspora with the Aadheenam’s spiritual and charitable activities.

Rooted in the lineage of the Kailaya Meikandar Guru Parambarai, the Aadheenam continues to serve through temple preservation, educational institutions, and social service initiatives.

We invite all devotees, Tamil families, and cultural organizations across the United States to join us in sustaining this sacred heritage.
